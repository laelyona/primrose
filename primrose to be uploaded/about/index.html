  ABOUT

  our story

A place to breathe.

 Primrose, since its establishments in January 2010, is known by the coffee importers of U.S.A Japan, Germany, France, China and Korea just to mention the few. In addition to its provision of specialty coffee of organic nature of Harar, Yirgacheffe, Sidamo Lekempti and Djimmah origin, its ethical business conduct and strict observance to the delivery schedule made its customers accept the highly competitive price that it offers and enjoys the benefits. The highest regard that it gives to the promotion of quality Ethiopian Arabica coffee most importantly enabled it to perform a quality conscious business in the global market. It′s well equipped coffee – cup – tasting center with its professionals are among others for its provision of specialty coffee of various types. The company′s strategy is to be loyal and friendly to the healthy environmental and social factors while conducting its business and to be cooperative in preventing any hazards that harm their sustainability with governmental and non governmental agency.   

Values:

: Commitment to quality, excellence and professionalism.

: Trustworthiness, honesty and integrity.

: Customers focus.

: Social responsibility and sustainability.

  • Objective: To be referred as the most favorable coffee exporter company
  • Mission: We are committed to provide unique coffee and conduct coffee business through provision of quality and steady hand on delivery in operating as a one stop green coffee exporting company by prioritizing the need of our customers.
  • Goal: Providing sustainable and traceable coffee is the ultimate goal of our company
  • Vision: To provide top quality coffee that scores above 90+ and exceeds the expectations of our esteemed customers.
